# My Enigma | AI-Powered Pharma Supply Chain

**My Enigma** is a next-generation SaaS platform designed to solve the most critical challenges in the pharmaceutical supply chain: **inventory wastage, medicine expiry, and manual inefficiencies.** By combining real-time automation with predictive AI, we ensure that life-saving medications reach patients efficiently while protecting the bottom line of retailers and wholesalers.

---

### 🏥 The Problem: A Trillion-Dollar Waste
Pharmacists and wholesalers lose billions annually to:
*   **Expiry Losses:** Medications sitting on shelves until they are unsellable.
*   **Overstocking:** Capital tied up in slow-moving inventory due to poor demand visibility.
*   **Stockouts:** Missed revenue and patient risks when critical drugs aren't available.
*   **Manual Entry Errors:** Human error during billing and stock updates leading to regulatory fines.

---

### 🚀 Key Features

#### 🏥 For Retailers (The Smart Pharmacy)
*   **AI-Driven Replenishment:** Predictive ordering analyzes seasonal trends and local health data to stock exactly what you need.
*   **Zero-Waste Inventory:** Automated "Near-Expiry" alerts help you prioritize sales or initiate returns before medicines become dead stock.
*   **Live Stock Intelligence:** See exactly what distributors have in stock—no more "order and hope."

#### 🚚 For Distributors (The Autonomous Warehouse)
*   **Autonomous Billing:** Orders from My Enigma push directly to your billing software with **ML-based error detection**.
*   **Demand Forecasting:** Machine Learning predicts market surges, allowing you to optimize stock levels and reduce holding costs.
*   **Real-time Transparency:** Reduce order cancellations by providing retailers with 100% accurate, live inventory data.

#### 💼 For Sales Executives (The Mobile Office)
*   **Predictive Insights:** Identify at-risk accounts and outstanding payment alerts before they become a problem.
*   **Smart Control:** Full visibility into retailer performance and inventory health via a dedicated mobile app.

### 🤖 Futuristic Intelligence
